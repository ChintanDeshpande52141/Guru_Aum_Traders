Our Story

What began as a simple idea turned into a journey of trust, tradition, and taste.

In 1970, we started with a mission to bring pure, farm-fresh products to every household. Coming from a family of farmers, we knew the value of honest produce and the stories behind every grain.

What began in a small village warehouse is now a trusted source of naturally grown grains, jaggery, and more — delivered straight to your door. Along the way, we’ve built strong relationships with farmers and customers alike.

Our goal has always been simple: support local, promote purity, and serve with integrity. Today, our journey continues, powered by your trust and the hard work of countless hands behind the scenes.
